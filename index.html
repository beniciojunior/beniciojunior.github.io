About Our App

Interacting the Scripture has never been easier. Viewing notes directly from the text, adding reactions to your favorite verses and easy navigation are some of the advantages you will find in this app.

Notes

Adding notes to verses has never been more practical. All notes added will appear directly below the verse in question. If you don't want to visualize it, just hide all the notes by pressing just one button.

Customization

Customize your content by adding notes and highlights with your favorite color, reactions using emojis and also choosing your favorite theme to the app.

iCloud Sync

All notes, highlights and reactions are automatically synced via iCloud. With this you can use different devices with the same iCloud account and your content will be synchronized between them automatically.
